Our Journey

From Idea to
Institution

What started as a bold idea to reinvent how the UAE accesses food has grown into a multi-brand food innovation group touching every layer of the supply chain.

🌱
Foundation · 2018

The Vision is Planted

Eco Choice Group was founded in Dubai with a single vision: build transparent, sustainable, and scalable food businesses that serve the UAE's growing and diverse population. Eco Choice General Trading LLC was the first entity — the seed from which the group grew.

🏗️
Expansion · 2019–2020

Building the Supply Chain

Al Wholesale was launched as a dedicated B2B food wholesale platform, connecting thousands of food businesses across the UAE to premium suppliers. Simultaneously, Eco Choice Meat Products LLC was established to control quality at the processing level.

🧑‍🍳
Consumer Brands · 2021–2022

Reaching the End Consumer

The group extended its reach directly to consumers with Al Chef To Home — a premium marinated and ready-to-cook meat delivery brand. Emirate Farms was established to strengthen local agricultural roots and champion food sovereignty in the UAE.

🍽️
Restaurant & Dining · 2022–2023

Opening Restaurant Concepts

Meat & Bites Restaurant LLC was launched as a premium dining experience. O'Bites, the UAE's creative O-shaped bite concept, opened at Deira City Centre, bringing a unique and fun food experience rooted in local culture and global flavors.

🔥
Innovation · 2024–Present

Fuego, Go-si & The Future

Fuego arrived with bold, fire-inspired flavors, and Go-si introduced a tech-enabled food discovery and delivery model. Today, Eco Choice Group is a fully integrated food ecosystem — and we're only getting started.

Direction

Vision & Mission

🔭

Our Vision

To be the UAE's most trusted and innovative food group — an ecosystem that serves as the backbone of the region's food security, connecting farms to consumers through ethical, transparent, and scalable systems that nourish both people and the planet.

We envision a future where every meal consumed in the UAE traces back to a system that is responsible, efficient, and built with integrity.

🎯

Our Mission

To build, grow, and operate a portfolio of high-impact food brands that span the full value chain — from climate-resilient farms to innovative restaurant concepts — while embedding sustainability, quality, and community impact into every decision we make.

We exist to make great food more accessible, transparent, and sustainable for the communities we serve across the Emirates and beyond.
