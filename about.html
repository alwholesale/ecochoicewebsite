Our Journey

From Seed to
Ecosystem

A bold idea to reimagine how the UAE accesses food has grown into a multi-brand food innovation group spanning the entire supply chain.

Foundation · 2018

The Vision is Planted

Eco Choice Group founded in Dubai with one vision: build transparent, sustainable, and scalable food businesses for the UAE's diverse population. Eco Choice General Trading LLC was the seed — the origin of the entire ecosystem.

Growth · 2019–2022

Building the Supply Chain

Al Wholesale launched as a dynamic B2B food platform connecting buyers and sellers with real-time pricing. Eco Choice Meat Products LLC established to control quality from processing to shelf. A fleet of 22 vehicles deployed for reliable UAE-wide delivery.

Consumer Expansion · 2023

Reaching Every Home & Table

Al Chef To Home launched — delivering expertly marinated, ready-to-cook Halal products to modern homes across online and offline platforms. Now available in Carrefour, Nesto, Ansar Group, Safari Mall, West Zone, Al Madina, and Day to Day.

Farm & QSR · 2024

Local Roots, Bold Concepts

Emirate Farms established to champion UAE food sovereignty through climate-resilient local agriculture. O'Bites — the UAE's first Micro-QSR concept — opened with globally inspired bites from locally sourced ingredients, ready in 60 seconds.

Innovation · 2025–2026

Fuego, Liquid Eggs & Manufacturing

Fuego launched in 2025 — exclusive private dining with authentic Mexican roots and Texan soul. Emirates Liquid Egg Project and Al Wholesale Centralised Manufacturing are arriving in 2026, further expanding our innovation-driven ecosystem.

Direction

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To transform the global food value chain with authentic, innovative, and sustainable concepts — establishing Eco Choice Group as the UAE's most trusted food ecosystem and a beacon of food security for the region and the world.

We envision a future where every meal traces back to a system that is responsible, efficient, and built with genuine integrity — from UAE roots to global impact.

Our Mission

To scale sustainable food and lifestyle concepts that drive authenticity, food security, and community impact — delivering seamless, fast, and value-driven experiences from field to fork.

We exist to make exceptional food accessible, transparent, and sustainable for every community we serve — through innovation, integrity, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

"We're not just creating food businesses — we're building scalable concepts that reimagine the value chain from field to fork through efficient, transparent, and sustainable systems."
