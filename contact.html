Quick Answers

Frequently Asked
Questions

Do you offer B2B wholesale pricing?

+
Yes! Through Al Wholesale, we offer competitive B2B pricing for restaurants, hotels, supermarkets, and institutional buyers. Contact us with your volume requirements and we'll provide a tailored quote.

Are your products Halal certified?

+
Absolutely. All products across Eco Choice Group — including all meat, poultry, and food items — are 100% Halal certified to the highest UAE standards. This is non-negotiable across our entire portfolio.

Do you deliver across all Emirates?

+
Our B2B distribution through Al Wholesale covers all 7 Emirates. For D2C delivery (Al Chef To Home), we currently serve Dubai with expansion planned across the UAE. Contact us for specific route availability.

Can I become a supplier to Eco Choice Group?

+
We welcome quality supplier applications. Please reach out via our contact form with details about your products, certifications, and capacity. Our procurement team will review your application and follow up.

Is franchise opportunity available?

+
We are actively exploring franchise opportunities for select brands including O'Bites and Fuego. Please get in touch via our contact form selecting "Franchise Opportunity" and our partnerships team will connect with you.

How can I order from Al Chef To Home?

+
Visit alcheftohome.com to browse and order online. You can also call (04) 357 0320 or WhatsApp 050 687 0224 for orders and enquiries. We deliver across Dubai.
