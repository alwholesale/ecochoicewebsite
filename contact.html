Quick Answers

Frequently Asked
Questions

Do you offer B2B wholesale pricing?

+
Yes. Through Al Wholesale we offer competitive B2B pricing for restaurants, hotels, supermarkets, and institutional buyers. Contact us with your volume requirements and we'll provide a tailored quote with transparent real-time pricing.

Are all your products Halal certified?

+
Absolutely. Every product across all ECO Choice Group brands — including all meat, poultry, and food items — is 100% Halal certified to the highest UAE standards. This is a non-negotiable commitment across our entire portfolio.

What is Al Chef To Home and how do I order?

+
Al Chef To Home delivers expertly marinated, ready-to-cook Halal meats to your home. Visit alcheftohome.com or call (04) 357 0320. Also available in Carrefour, Nesto, Ansar Group, Safari Mall, West Zone, Al Madina, and Day to Day.

How does your delivery fleet work?

+
Our fleet of 22 vehicles ensures fast, reliable, and temperature-controlled deliveries across all 7 Emirates. Al Wholesale's platform enables seamless B2B order tracking and real-time visibility — promoting efficiency and reliability for all our business clients.

Can I book O'Bites Food Truck for an event?

+
Yes! The O'Bites Food Truck brings fresh, delicious O-shaped bites to your events, parties, and gatherings across the UAE. Contact us via this form selecting "O'Bites — Food Truck Booking" and our team will provide availability and pricing details.

Are franchise opportunities available?

+
We are actively exploring franchise opportunities for select brands including O'Bites and Fuego. Please reach out via our contact form selecting "Franchise Opportunity" and our partnerships team will connect with you to discuss further.
