What We Do

End-to-End
Food Services

From farm to fork, wholesale to dining — we operate across every critical layer of the UAE food ecosystem, creating value at each step of the supply chain.

Service Pillars

Six Core Capabilities

Six integrated service verticals that work together to create a robust, future-ready food ecosystem for the UAE.

🌐

Food Trading & Import

Strategic sourcing, importing, and distribution of premium food products across FMCG, bulk commodities, specialty foods, and fresh produce.

  • Global supplier network management
  • Import compliance & documentation
  • Competitive B2B pricing
  • Custom sourcing on demand
  • Quality inspection protocols
🏪

Wholesale Distribution

High-volume B2B food distribution connecting suppliers with restaurants, hotels, supermarkets, and institutional buyers across the UAE.

  • Next-day delivery capability
  • Cold chain logistics management
  • Bulk pricing & contract agreements
  • HORECA-focused solutions
  • Inventory management support
🌾

Local Farming & Agriculture

Investment in UAE-based farming using climate-adaptive methods to grow fresh produce locally and reduce dependence on food imports.

  • Hydroponic & vertical farming
  • Open-field sustainable cultivation
  • Farm-to-table direct supply
  • UAE food sovereignty support
  • Seasonal & year-round produce
🥩

Meat Processing & Value-Add

End-to-end Halal meat processing, marination, packaging, and distribution — delivering premium quality from source to shelf.

  • Halal-certified processing facility
  • Custom cuts for HORECA
  • Marination & flavoring services
  • Vacuum-packed & retail-ready
  • Whole lamb & bulk order handling
🚴

D2C Delivery & Food Tech

Direct-to-consumer food delivery platforms that leverage technology to bring premium, convenient food experiences to UAE households.

  • Same-day & scheduled delivery
  • Digital-first ordering experience
  • Subscription meal plans
  • Multi-brand delivery integration
  • Aggregator partnerships (Talabat, Deliveroo)
🍽️

Restaurant & Dining Concepts

Innovative, scalable restaurant brands designed to deliver exceptional dining experiences across fine casual, QSR, and food court formats.

  • Premium halal dining concepts
  • QSR & food court operations
  • Event catering & private dining
  • Franchise development pipeline
  • Menu innovation & R&D
