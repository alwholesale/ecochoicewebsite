What We Do

End-to-End
Food Services

From farm to fork, wholesale to dining — we operate across every critical layer of the UAE food ecosystem, creating value and trust at each step of the supply chain.

Service Pillars

Six Core Capabilities

Six integrated service verticals that work together to create a robust, future-ready food ecosystem for the UAE and beyond.

Food Trading & Import

Strategic global sourcing and distribution — importing from multiple sources to guarantee quality, freshness, and consistency across the UAE.

  • Multi-source global import strategy
  • FMCG, bulk commodity & specialty foods
  • Import compliance & documentation
  • Quality inspection at every stage
  • Custom sourcing on demand

B2B Wholesale Platform

Al Wholesale's dynamic online platform connecting buyers and sellers with real-time interactive pricing charts and seamless operations.

  • Real-time transparent pricing charts
  • HORECA, wholesale & retail channels
  • 22-vehicle fleet for reliable delivery
  • Custom bulk pricing & contracts
  • Nationwide UAE distribution

Local Farming & Agriculture

UAE-grown produce through climate-resilient farming — reducing import dependency and championing local food sovereignty for the nation.

  • Hydroponic & climate-adaptive farming
  • Fresh vegetables, herbs & seasonal produce
  • Farm-to-table direct supply
  • UAE food security alignment
  • Year-round production capability

Meat Processing & Value-Add

State-of-the-art Halal processing facility producing premium meat for HORECA and retail with custom packaging and private label options.

  • Halal-certified processing factory
  • Custom cuts for restaurants & retail
  • Marination & ready-to-cook services
  • Private label & custom packaging
  • Cold chain UAE-wide distribution

D2C Delivery & Retail

Al Chef To Home delivers marinated and ready-to-cook meats direct to modern homes — online, on major platforms, and in leading retail chains.

  • Online ordering & same-day delivery
  • Available in Carrefour, Nesto, Ansar Group
  • Safari Mall, West Zone, Al Madina & more
  • Talabat, Deliveroo, Keeta & Noon Food
  • Subscription & bulk home delivery

Restaurant & Dining Concepts

From UAE's first Micro-QSR to exclusive private dining — our restaurant concepts deliver innovative food experiences across multiple formats.

  • O'Bites — UAE's First Micro-QSR
  • Fuego — exclusive Mex-Tex private dining
  • O'Bites Food Truck for events
  • Catering & corporate events
  • Franchise development pipeline
