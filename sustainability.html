Framework

Four Pillars of
Sustainable Action

Our sustainability framework is built around four integrated pillars that guide how we operate, grow, and make a difference.

🌱

Environmental Stewardship

We recognise that the food industry is one of the largest contributors to environmental stress. At Eco Choice, we take active steps to reduce our footprint and protect the ecosystems we depend on.

✓
Investment in hydroponic & low-water farming techniques
✓
Cold chain optimisation to reduce energy consumption
✓
Responsible packaging transition across all brands
✓
Food waste minimisation at processing & retail levels
🛡️

UAE Food Security

We are deeply invested in strengthening the UAE's food security infrastructure — building local supply chains that are resilient to global disruptions and climate change.

✓
Emirate Farms: growing fresh produce locally in the UAE
✓
Supporting UAE Vision 2031 food security targets
✓
Diversified supplier base to reduce single-source risk
✓
Strategic food reserve capability through Al Wholesale
🤝

Community & Social Impact

Our businesses exist because of the communities they serve. We measure success not just in revenue, but in lives improved, livelihoods created, and communities nourished.

✓
Employment creation across multiple UAE sectors
✓
Supporting small food businesses through Al Wholesale
✓
Making nutritious food accessible & affordable
✓
Cultural food diversity through our restaurant concepts
💡

Innovation for Good

We believe that the most powerful sustainability tool is innovation — building better systems, smarter processes, and more responsible food businesses that scale without harming the planet.

✓
Tech-enabled delivery reducing food transit waste
✓
Data-driven demand forecasting to minimise overstock
✓
R&D investment in sustainable food processing
✓
Scalable food concepts designed for responsible growth
Global Alignment

UN Sustainable Development
Goals We Champion

Our work aligns with key UN Sustainable Development Goals — guiding our long-term impact strategy.

SDG 2
ZERO HUNGER

Through Al Wholesale, Emirate Farms, and our distribution network, we work to ensure consistent access to nutritious, affordable food across the UAE and support food security at a national level.

SDG 12
RESPONSIBLE CONSUMPTION

We design our businesses to minimise food waste, use responsible packaging, and source ingredients through ethical supply chains — making sustainability part of everyday food decisions.

SDG 13
CLIMATE ACTION

Emirate Farms invests in climate-adaptive farming that works within the UAE's extreme environment, reducing food miles and building resilience against global climate-driven food supply disruptions.

SDG 8
DECENT WORK & GROWTH

Our group provides quality employment across multiple sectors — from farm workers and warehouse staff to chefs and delivery professionals — creating inclusive economic opportunity in the UAE.

SDG 11
SUSTAINABLE CITIES

Our restaurant and delivery concepts are designed around urban food access, reducing the burden of food insecurity in dense city environments and making great food reachable for all UAE residents.

SDG 17
PARTNERSHIPS FOR GOALS

We actively build partnerships with farmers, suppliers, NGOs, and government bodies to amplify our impact — because transforming food systems requires collaboration at every level.
