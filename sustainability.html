    UN SDGs We
    Champion

    Our work aligns with key UN Sustainable Development Goals — guiding our long-term impact strategy and business decisions.

    SDG 2
    Zero Hunger

    Through Al Wholesale and Emirate Farms, we work to ensure consistent access to nutritious, affordable food across all 7 Emirates and support national food security at scale.

    SDG 12
    Responsible Consumption

    We design businesses that minimise food waste, use responsible supply chains, and make real-time pricing data available so food trade is more efficient and less wasteful.

    SDG 13
    Climate Action

    Emirate Farms invests in climate-adaptive farming for the UAE's environment — reducing food miles and building resilience against global climate-driven food supply disruptions.

    SDG 8
    Decent Work & Growth

    Our group provides quality employment across farming, processing, wholesale, delivery, and dining — creating inclusive economic opportunities throughout the UAE food sector.

    SDG 11
    Sustainable Cities

    Our QSR and delivery concepts are designed around urban food access — making great, nutritious food reachable and affordable for all UAE urban residents daily.

    SDG 17
    Partnerships for Goals

    We build partnerships with farmers, suppliers, retailers, and government bodies to amplify our impact — because transforming food systems requires genuine collaboration at every level.
